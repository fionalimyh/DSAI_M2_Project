📈 Delivery vs Revenue Correlation
Each dot is one zip code region. The dashed line is the linear trend — its slope shows how much revenue changes per extra delivery day. R² measures how strongly delivery time alone explains revenue differences (0 = no relationship, 1 = perfect).

Composite score (0–100) = 60% revenue + 40% delivery speed, both normalized to the current filtered data. Shows the top 10 and bottom 10 regions. Scores are relative — they shift when filters change.
